Description

Prism Shell is a high intensity sci-fi action game with intuitive touch controls. Hold your finger on the screen to fire at the alien hordes, and tap glowing points to glide across the battlefield in your high-powered hover tank. Dodge alien attacks, shift into better firing positions, and collect currency for upgrades. You are Earth’s last chance for survival. Can you and Major Beretta pilot the Prism Shell to victory?

Features

  • You’ll be fighting the aliens in no time with easy-to-learn touch controls
  • Earn in-game currency to power up your tank – no in-app purchases required!
  • Take control of the alien-destroying laser to turn the tables on your foes
  • Multiple aliens to fight with different behavior types and attack methods

History

The first version of Prism Shell was created for a 2013 Tizen Devlab and Hack event in New York. The goal of the game was to create a fun, intuitive space shooter game without onscreen controls. The game had such great gameplay and polish that the judges were surprised it was actually made in 24 hours.

After winning the event’s runner-up prize, the team continued to work on Prism Shell with the intention of releasing it for Tizen, a new mobile operating system. The game grew into a much larger project with planned in-app purchases and multiple tanks.

Unfortunately, Tizen’s release date was pushed back, so the team decided to release Prism Shell on Android and iOS instead. In order to ship the game faster, the team decided to scale the game back down to its core differentiators.
